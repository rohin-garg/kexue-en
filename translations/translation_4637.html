[Snapshots] Canton Tower at My Doorstep

By 苏剑林 | October 13, 2017

At the end of last semester, I changed dorms and moved to the 7th floor. My doorstep happens to face the Canton Tower. Last night, the weather was clear and quite cool, so I felt inspired to take some photos. I used a tripod + Huawei P9, using "Super Night View" mode with an exposure of about 30 seconds (you can right-click to open links for high-definition versions).
