Welcome to Register for an @spaces.ac.cn Email (Update!)

By 苏剑林 | September 12, 2009

Note: Currently, QQ domain mailboxes no longer allow the addition of new accounts, so applications are suspended. (February 17, 2020)

Introduction #

Scientific Spaces, in collaboration with Tencent, has launched a QQ mailbox service with the suffix @spaces.ac.cn. QQ users are welcome to apply for registration.

Tencent can be said to be getting stronger and stronger. Previously, it has provided instant messaging software (QQ), electronic mail, personal spaces, and other popular features. Now, it also provides a personalized domain email service. As long as you have a domain name, you can use your own domain's email, and this email space is your current QQ mailbox. This feature is similar to those provided by Google and Microsoft; however, in China, Microsoft's email is extraordinarily slow, and Google does not support domains other than .com, .net, and .org. Therefore, QQ Mail is still better. This site has used the domain spaces.ac.cn to open this personalized email service.

Meaning #

Therefore, if you do not have your own domain, why not use this space's domain spaces.ac.cn as your personalized QQ mailbox? This can better display your scientific taste. Because—

The domain name Spaces.Ac.Cn: "Spaces" stands for "outer space" or "space," while "Ac" represents "Academic," and "Cn," needless to say, is "China."

Using this email, which contains "Space," "Academic," and "China," can better reflect your unique scientific life. Moreover, email names can be chosen arbitrarily, so there is no need to worry about them being pre-registered by others!

Application #

This site will provide free activation of personalized emails like abc@spaces.ac.cn for some QQ friends. Note that this email is merely an alias for your QQ mailbox; therefore, emails sent to this address are delivered to your QQ mailbox. Consequently, there is only an account name and no separate password; the password is your original QQ mailbox password.

The prerequisite for application is: You must have a QQ account, and this account must have already activated the QQ mailbox service. If you do not have one, please go to http://reg.qq.com to apply for a QQ account.

Currently, the only way to apply is by sending an email. Send your desired email account and your QQ number (must be a QQ number) to bojone@spaces.ac.cn.

Other #

After your application is approved, you will receive an activation email. Simply accept the activation directly. You can then also log in to our personalized mailbox via this login page address: http://mail.spaces.ac.cn

Of course, if you already have your own domain, you can use your own domain as an email suffix. For details, please see: http://domain.mail.qq.com/
